Key features to consider in fleet
management software

Unified Data Integration

Bring together data from diagnostics, tracking, fuel consumption, and maintenance to provide a comprehensive view of your fleet’s performance.

Customizable Geofencing

Set up virtual perimeters and receive real-time alerts for vehicle movements, ensuring better oversight and operational efficiency.

Unlimited Access for All

The ability to accommodate unlimited users means that everyone involved has access to the software, ensuring transparency and collaboration with real-time data and reports.

Full Fleet Transparency

Managers should have access to real-time dashboards that provide detailed insights into vehicle locations, status updates, driver behaviour, and operational metrics.

Instant Communication Tools

Facilitate real-time communication between teams, allowing dispatchers to assign tasks and receive updates from drivers instantly.

Simple and Accessible

A user-friendly design of efficient fleet management software ensures quick adoption, reduces training time, and boosts productivity by providing easy access to critical information.

Offers more than location tracking

Go beyond GPS tracking with features like predictive maintenance, fuel management, driver monitoring, and compliance tools to boost productivity.
