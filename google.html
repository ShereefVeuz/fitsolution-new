Google Workspace Setup and Migration

We handle the swift and secure setup of Google Workspace, ensuring a transition with ease for your organization. Our team guarantees a smooth migration of your crucial data from your existing systems to Google Workspace, so you’re set for success from the start.

User Training

We empower your team with in-depth training on how to fully leverage the features of Google Workspace. Our tailored sessions and learning resources are designed provide comprehensive training sessions and resources to bring your employees up to speed.

Custom Domain Configuration

Enhance your organization’s professionalism by configuring custom domains for email and collaboration tools. Google Workspace enables you to personalize your domain, reinforcing your brand’s identity and credibility in every communication.

Email and Calendar Management

Take control of your email communications with Gmail and optimise scheduling with Google Calendar. Our solutions ensure that your team can easily share, manage, and collaborate on calendars, improving overall productivity.

Tools for Collaboration

For real-time collaboration on documents, spreadsheets, and presentations, use Google Drive, Docs, Sheets, and Slides. Use Meet and Google Chat for video conferencing and messaging to promote teamwork.

Security and Data Protection

With Google Workspace, we prioritize your data security. Our experts implement advanced security measures, including monitoring tools and customized settings, to ensure your sensitive information remains safe and compliant.

Support and Maintenance

Our dedicated team offers ongoing support to address any issues swiftly, ensuring your Google Workspace remains optimized. We handle system updates, backups, and proactive maintenance, giving you peace of mind as your organization grows.
