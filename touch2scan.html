FAQ

The Touch2Scan NFC card is a modern card that leverages Near Field Communication (NFC) technology to offer fast and easy interactions. With a simple tap, you can access a range of services, share information, or make transactions, all without the need for physical contact or complex setups.

The card operates using NFC technology, which allows it to communicate with compatible devices when they are in close proximity. To use it, simply tap the card against an NFC-enabled device.

Yes, the Touch2Scan NFC card is designed with security in mind. It employs encryption and secure protocols to ensure that any data transmitted between the card and the NFC-enabled device is protected.

The Touch2Scan NFC card is compatible with any device that supports NFC technology. This includes most modern smartphones and tablets.

The Touch2Scan NFC card does not have a battery. It uses passive NFC technology, which means it draws power from the NFC-enabled device it’s interacting with. As a result, the card is always ready to use and does not require charging or battery replacement.
